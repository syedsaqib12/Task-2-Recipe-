Halwa Puri Recipe

Ingredients

Puri

  • 1/4 cup Oil
  • 200 gm Gram boiled
  • 250 gm Semolina
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin

Aloo ki bhujia

  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek
  • 1 tbsp Red pepper powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste

Cholay

  • 1 cup Ghee
  • 1 pinch Yellow color
  • 20 gm Coconut
  • 20 gm Raisins

Halwa

  • 20 gm Cashew
  • 1 pinch Yellow color
  • 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
